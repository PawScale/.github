## Hi there 👋

Welcome to **PawScale** - Innovative Solutions for Pet Care and Wellness!

### 🙋‍♀️ About PawScale

PawScale is a community-driven organization dedicated to building cutting-edge tools, libraries, and applications that revolutionize pet care and management. We're passionate about helping pet owners, veterinarians, and pet care professionals better care for their furry, feathered, and scaly companions.

Our mission is to make pet health and wellness accessible, affordable, and data-driven through open-source technology and community collaboration.
